About Me

I'm a product designer with a strong UX core, specializing in breaking down complex workflows and data-heavy problems into intuitive, scalable solutions. My approach to design is grounded in curiosity, logic, and structure—ensuring that every interface I create not only looks great but also functions seamlessly for users.
