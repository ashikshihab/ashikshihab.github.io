Overview

ScrapeHero is a full-service data company trusted by Fortune 50 enterprises and 14,000+ customers globally. They offer web scraping services, a prebuilt API marketplace, and a self-serve data store, three distinct product lines serving very different types of buyers.

I led the complete redesign of ScrapeHero's digital ecosystem, working across all three platforms to fix structural and UX problems. My work spanned user research, information architecture, interaction design, and full design-to-development delivery for the entire ecosystem.

Key Challenges

The core challenge was the same across all three surfaces: ScrapeHero's products were technically superior, but the interface made it difficult for buyers to reach them.

Challenge 1: Fragmented Navigation & IA

The marketing website lacked a coherent navigation structure. Each page felt different from the next, and the information architecture was fragmented: users were bouncing between pages without finding a clear path to the specific data service they needed.

Visually, the experience was inconsistent. Each page felt disconnected from the next, making it difficult for enterprise buyers to trust and navigate the product offering effectively.

Restructured navigation and information architecture across the main website.

Approach: I audited the full site and established a scalable navigation system built around the buyer's journey: from awareness to evaluation to action.

I introduced consistent page templates and repositioned key CTAs to be visible at critical decision moments. This made the site navigable even for users with no prior knowledge of ScrapeHero.

Challenge 2: Marketplace Discovery & Friction

ScrapeHero Cloud hosts a diverse library of scrapers, but poor filtering and categorization made discovery difficult. Users frequently struggled to find the right tool for their use case.

Additionally, the "Free Plan" was buried, causing potential users to leave without realizing they could start for free. High-intent users also faced detours in the subscription flow, leading to drop-offs at the most critical moment.

Redesigned marketplace with improved filtering and prominent "Free Plan" surfacing.

Approach: I revamped the marketplace with improved categorization and multi-dimensional filters, significantly reducing the time required to find a relevant scraper.

To reduce subscription friction, I used session recordings to identify and remove unnecessary steps in the signup flow. I also surfaced the Free Plan prominently to lower the barrier to entry and drive product-led growth.

Challenge 3: E-commerce UX in the Data Store

The Data Store had structural issues from listing to checkout. All datasets were grouped regardless of industry or type, and listing pages lacked the key metadata buyers needed to evaluate them.

Product pages also failed to highlight historical data, the most requested feature by customers. The checkout flow was cumbersome, making simple transactions unnecessarily difficult for users.

Redesigned Data Store featuring structured filters and metadata-rich listing cards.

Approach: I redesigned the store based on research into what buyers look for most. I introduced industry-standard filters and metadata indicators, such as freshness and coverage, directly on the listing cards.

The Product Detail Pages were rebuilt to lead with historical data, and the checkout flow was streamlined to remove transactional friction, creating a much faster path to purchase.

Design System Evolution

To ensure consistency, I developed a unified design system that scaled across the Marketing, Marketplace, and Store platforms. This was the first shared UI foundation in the company's history.

Efficiency Gain: By standardizing components like data tables and filter bars, the team can now launch new product categories 40% faster while maintaining a premium brand aesthetic.

Overall Impact

Unified Ecosystem

Transformed fragmented products into a cohesive ecosystem. The platform is now navigable through logic, drastically improving how leads orient themselves.

Increased Conversion

By streamlining checkout and signup flows using analytics, we successfully reached a higher conversion rate for high-intent users across the marketplace and store.

Brand Trust

Established a consistent visual identity and UX across every touchpoint, building immediate trust with enterprise buyers through transparency and professional quality.
